Our Metro Services: Convenient, Reliable, and Accessible

At MetroConnect, we are committed to providing a seamless travel experience for all our passengers. From well-connected routes to affordable fares, our services are designed to make your journey as smooth as possible. Explore below to learn more about what we offer.

Metro Routes & Timetable

MetroConnect provides an extensive network of routes, covering key areas across the city to ensure seamless connectivity. Whether you're commuting for work, leisure, or education, our metro lines are designed to save you time and reduce traffic congestion. We pride ourselves on our punctual services, with trains running at frequent intervals to ensure minimal waiting times. You can easily plan your journey using our up-to-date timetable, which provides real-time information on train arrival and departure. Keep track of any schedule changes through our website or mobile app, ensuring that you stay informed at all times.

Affordable Fares for Every Journey

At MetroConnect, we believe that public transportation should be affordable and accessible to everyone. We offer a range of fare options tailored to different travel needs. Whether you're a daily commuter or an occasional traveler, our fare structure is designed to be economical. You can purchase single-ride tickets or opt for special discounts on round-trip fares. Additionally, students, senior citizens, and disabled passengers can avail themselves of concessions that make travel even more affordable. With multiple ways to purchase tickets, including online, through our app, or at the station, we make it simple for you to get where you need to go.

Upcoming MetroConnect Projects: Expanding Pune's Transit Future

MetroConnect is constantly expanding to improve the service experience for our passengers. Several exciting projects are currently in progress to extend the metro lines and make travel even more convenient across Pune and Pimpri Chinchwad. The upcoming projects include extensions to the existing metro lines, such as the PCMC to Nigdi extension on Line 1 and the Swargate to Katraj extension. These expansions will connect more neighborhoods, reduce travel time, and make metro journeys smoother and more efficient. With modern, safe, and comfortable metro stations under development, these projects aim to further enhance the lifestyle and convenience of our passengers. Stay updated with us as we continue to grow our services and provide a better metro experience for all.
